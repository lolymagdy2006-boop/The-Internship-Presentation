My Internship Journey at UFE WorX

During my internship at UFE WorX, I gained practical experience in digital marketing, content creation, social media management, event promotion, and professional communication. This website showcases my main responsibilities, projects, skills acquired, challenges encountered, and the impact of this experience on my professional development.

Core Responsibilities

📱 Social Media Management

I participated in managing the company's social media presence by publishing content on LinkedIn and Instagram, preparing captions, scheduling posts using Buffer, and organizing content calendars. This responsibility helped me understand how organizations maintain an active and professional digital presence.

🎨 Content Creation

I created marketing content for different platforms, including social media posts, promotional materials, captions, and Canva designs. I also contributed ideas during brainstorming sessions and helped prepare content for campaigns and events.

🎥 YouTube Management

I contributed to the development of the UFE WorX YouTube channel by creating titles, descriptions, thumbnails, timelines, and chapters. This allowed me to learn more about content organization and video platform management.

📢 Event Marketing

I supported the promotion of the UFE WorX Job Fair through social media content, event communication, and promotional materials. This experience showed me how marketing strategies can be applied to increase awareness and engagement around events.

🤝 Public Relations

I assisted with communication activities, interview coordination, announcements, and press release preparation. These tasks strengthened my professional communication skills and introduced me to the importance of public relations.
